New Mount Namao Explorer Pass

Travel by Daet Railway to Hamoraon Station and ride the Mount Namao Cable Car with one convenient ticket. The new pass includes round-trip cable car ride and exclusive discounts at the souvenir shop.

Petition Calls for the Return of Limited Express "Calaguas"

Daet Railway Fanclub has launched a petition urging the quick reinstatement of the Limited Express Calaguas between Daet and Naga.

Steam Locomotive "Pinya" Set to Return

The only operational passenger steam locomotive in the Philippines is now 80% restored. Testing and trial runs are scheduled before it returns to service next year.
